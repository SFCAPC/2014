  • Our
    Families

    EVERY FAMILY WANTS TO PROTECT THEIR CHILDREN

    Our new program of intensive services, called Integrated Family Services, helps vulnerable families in building their protective factors — protective factors proven to create strong families and prevent abuse. By using the protective factors as a standard set of goals, we are able to create individualized service plans for each family.

    In 2014, we expanded this program from a pilot of 12 to more than 60 at-risk families. Our personalized, wraparound approach along with our (early) positive results have garnered local and national interest. In the coming year, we will explore how to share our model and our learnings.

    Of the families who participated in intensive services for six months

    72% improved their overall ability to protect their children

    71% improved basic parenting knowledge in area of concern
